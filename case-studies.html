All the Case Study element for the Open Museum were designed to be explored without providing instructions. How the audience is to interact with them is self-explanatory and lead to exploring the case study artwork in unique ways that are as entertaining as they are engaging. For those seeking further insights, each Case Study Element is accompanied by an Engagement Application. This application not only introduces visitors to novel digital experiences but also imparts valuable information regarding the showcased artwork. Additionally, it elucidates how the Case Study Element integrates findings from the color research conducted by the PERCEIVE project.
